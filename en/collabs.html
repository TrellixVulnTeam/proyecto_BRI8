Here are just a few brands I've been lucky enough to work with..

Club Med

This was such an amazing trip, getting to experience the new all inclusive hotel for 10 days in Les Arcs Panorama, France. Located in the middle of the mountain for ski in and out, beautiful views from the balcony and so many activities prepared for us, by far this has been my favorite work trip.
❮ ❯

Ford

Introducing the new Maverick, they managed to combine everything I love in one campaign! I was told I had to fly an airplane at sunset by the beach, and I just can not put into words how much fun this trip was! The coolest thing was when I was already off work, a few more nights were included at the hotel so I was able to stay 2 more days at the beach to just enjoy myself and relax, it all felt just like a mini vacation.

Billabong

My favorite brand ever since I was a little girl, my 10 year old self would have not believed me if I told her I would be working for this brand as a young adult. Such a nice beachy, comfy, Australian brand, and I am forever grateful.
❮ ❯

Ultimate Ears

These speakers have traveled with me almost everywhere, music is something everyone loves, with this brand we have done such awesome campaigns, trips and events, and got to meet such amazing people.
❮ ❯

Sophia Jewelry

They managed to make me feel like I'm a fairy, mermaid, goddess. Just absolutely loved shooting and creating for this brand, it really felt as magical as it looks.
❮ ❯

Compania del Sol

I'm so lucky to be working with this brand, they have by far my favorite bikinis and work-out clothes ever.. the colors, the quality, they are to die for. I have so many of them, honestly I can never decide which one to wear. We've done soo many campaigns, and luckily aiming to work many more years together.
